## Requirement
Autonomous Company Operating System
Core Objective

Build an AI-powered Autonomous Company Operating System that allows a founder to focus on ideas, vision, and strategic direction while AI agents handle planning, coordination, execution, monitoring, reporting, and continuous improvement across the organization.

The founder should not need to manually manage projects, assign tasks, coordinate teams, track progress, or chase updates.

The system should operate as an intelligent executive team that converts ideas into execution and continuously drives company objectives forward.

Founder Experience

As the founder:

I provide ideas.
I define goals.
I approve major decisions.
I review progress.
I receive recommendations.
I give objectives,

Everything else should be orchestrated by the system.

Example:

Founder:
Build a deployment module for Sentra.

System:
Analyzes objective
Creates requirements
Creates architecture
Builds execution plan
Creates tasks
Assigns work
Tracks progress
Identifies blockers
Requests approvals
Monitors quality
Coordinates deployment
Updates documentation
Reports completion

The founder never manually manages the workflow.

Problem Being Solved

Founders spend too much time:

Managing projects
Assigning work
Following up on progress
Coordinating teams
Organizing information
Tracking dependencies
Reviewing status updates

These activities create operational overhead that limits growth.

The platform should eliminate this burden by acting as an autonomous management layer.

Vision

Create a self-operating digital company where:

The Founder sets direction.
AI Agents manage execution.
Work is automatically planned.
Tasks are automatically assigned.
Priorities are continuously optimized.
Risks are proactively identified.
Knowledge is automatically captured.
Progress is continuously reported.

The company should continue moving forward even when the founder is not actively involved.

What the System Must Do
1. Understand Founder Intent

When the founder submits:

Build customer onboarding system

The platform should automatically:

Determine scope
Create requirements
Generate architecture
Build roadmap
Estimate effort
Create milestones

without requiring manual planning.

2. Autonomous Work Allocation

The system should:

Identify required departments
Create objectives
Generate tasks
Assign agents
Manage dependencies
Rebalance workloads

automatically.

3. Continuous Company Monitoring

The system should constantly evaluate:

Product health
Project health
Revenue trends
Customer feedback
Team performance
Technical debt
Delivery risks

and surface only what requires founder attention.

4. Executive Briefing System

Every day the founder receives:

CEO Summary
Strategic opportunities
Risks
Product priorities
COO Summary
Operational status
Delivery health
Resource utilization
Project Summary
Progress
Blockers
Upcoming milestones
Company Summary
Goals achieved
Goals at risk
Recommended actions
5. Organizational Memory

Every decision is automatically stored:

Why it happened
Who approved it
Impact
Related projects
Future implications

The company becomes smarter over time.

6. Self-Improving Operations

The system should continuously:

Detect bottlenecks
Improve workflows
Reassign resources
Optimize priorities
Recommend process changes

without waiting for founder intervention.

Success Metric

The platform succeeds when the founder can simply say:

"Build this."

and the company automatically plans, executes, tracks, reports, learns, and improves until the objective is completed.

One-Sentence Vision

"An autonomous AI-powered company where the founder provides direction, and the organization plans, executes, learns, and grows with minimal operational involvement."
